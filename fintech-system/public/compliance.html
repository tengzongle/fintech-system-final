MAS Compliance

Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) Licensing

We comply with the Payment Services Act (PSA) by obtaining the necessary licenses from the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS). This includes:

  • Applying for and maintaining the appropriate license type (Standard Payment Institution License or Major Payment Institution License).
  • Ensuring ongoing compliance with MAS regulatory requirements, including reporting obligations and operational standards.

Empowering Financial Freedom

We adhere to the Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA) to protect user data by implementing the following measures:

  • Collecting, using, and disclosing personal data only with the user’s consent and for specified purposes.
  • Implementing robust data protection policies and procedures to safeguard user information from unauthorized access or disclosure.
  • Conducting regular reviews and updates to our data protection practices to ensure compliance with the PDPA.
